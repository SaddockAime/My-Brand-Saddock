Drone based powerline insulator inspection

In this project students developed a drone capable of detecting if a powerline insulator is polluted or not. This detection is based on images taken from a drone, as it flies.

This enables early warning to a power transmission or distribution utility company of an impending insulator failure due to the accumulation of dirt on it. Cleaning of insulators is a risky endeavor, which can pose significant risk to human operators engaged in this task, so the developed drone aims to eliminate such a risk.

The objective of this project is to design and implement a solar quadrotor that can be used for aerial photography with the longest flight duration possible in clear-sky and average wind speed.
